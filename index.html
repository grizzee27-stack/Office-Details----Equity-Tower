⬡ Streets, Area & Transport Connectivity

Address & Street Context
Primary address: SCBD Lot 9, Jl. Jend. Sudirman Kav. 52-53, RT.5/RW.3, Senayan, Kebayoran Baru, Jakarta Selatan 12190
Main frontage: Jl. Jenderal Sudirman — Jakarta's primary north-south CBD arterial; eight-lane divided highway, prestige address.
Southern access: Jl. Gatot Subroto — connects to south Jakarta and toll interchange; 5-minute drive via inner SCBD road.
Inner SCBD road system: Private internal roads managed by PT Danayasa Arthatama; low congestion within campus; free SCBD shuttle circulates.
Senopati access: Secondary entry from the south via Jl. Senopati — notably exempt from Jakarta's "ganjil-genap" (odd-even plate) traffic restriction, providing all-day access regardless of plate number.
Nearest toll road: Jakarta Inner Ring Road (~600m from SCBD perimeter) — quick access to Soekarno-Hatta Airport and all radial toll routes.
Administrative area: Senayan, Kebayoran Baru, Jakarta Selatan — South Jakarta per client brief. ✓ Correct jurisdiction.

MRT Note: MRT Istora Mandiri is ~500m from the SCBD perimeter and ~900m to Equity Tower's lobby entrance on Jl. Sudirman (approx. 8–12 min walk). The free SCBD internal shuttle bus effectively bridges this gap — the practical commute from MRT to building entrance is 10–15 min including shuttle wait. By comparison, SCBD is the strongest MRT-connected office precinct in South Jakarta: Istora Mandiri sits directly on the North-South MRT Line (Lebak Bulus ↔ Bundaran HI), connecting workers from Blok M, Senayan, Dukuh Atas, and Bundaran HI without a transfer. Commuter Line: Stasiun Sudirman (~3 km) accessible via MRT interchange at Dukuh Atas BNI.
